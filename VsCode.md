## 插件
### Power Mode
敲代码特效
```json
 //是否开启
  "powermode.enabled": true,
  //效果样式 "水花-particles","烟花-fireworks", "火焰-flames", "雪花-magic","幽灵-clippy", "激光-simple-rift", "大激光-exploding-rift",
  "powermode.presets": "particles",
  //时间间隔
  "powermode.comboTimeout": 5,
  //是否抖动
  "powermode.enableShake": false,
```
